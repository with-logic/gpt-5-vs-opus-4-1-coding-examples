Stories of Hope

A New Beginning for Amara

"Before the well, I walked 3 hours every day. Now, I go to school."

In the village of Kolo, clean water was a luxury. Thanks to your donations, we installed a solar-powered pump that serves 500 families. Amara, age 12, is now top of her class.

Empowering Communities

"We don't just have water; we have health and time to build our businesses."

The Women's Cooperative in San Pedro used the time saved from water collection to start a textile business. Income in the village has risen by 40% in just one year.
