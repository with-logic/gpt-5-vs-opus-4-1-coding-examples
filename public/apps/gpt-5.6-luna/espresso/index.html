01 / The lab

Not another box of coffee.

There is a difference between having good equipment and knowing what to do with it. We give you both: the tools, the beans, and an expert in your corner when the shot goes sideways.

01On loan

A proper home bench

Rotate through prosumer equipment as your palate evolves. Grinder, brewer, scale, and the right accessories—delivered to your door.

02Every month

Green coffee with a point of view

Thoughtful lots from small producers, chosen for what they teach you—not what looks good on a shelf.

03When you need it

A trained eye on call

Weekly office hours and one-on-one calibration. Get unstuck quickly, then get back to the part you came for.

02 / The practice

Six months to a signature shot.

Not a course you finish and forget. A measured arc of experiments, feedback, and small victories that compound into instinct.
