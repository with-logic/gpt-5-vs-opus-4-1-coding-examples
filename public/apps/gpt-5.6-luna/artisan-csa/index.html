01 / The seasonal share

A box with a season in it.

We plan each box around what’s growing beautifully right now. Expect abundance, a few surprises, and a recipe or two to make it sing.

Mar—May

Spring

Tender greens & bright beginnings.

Try: radishes, snap peas, nettles, baby lettuce & rhubarb.

Jun—Aug

Summer

Sun-warm, juicy & generous.

Try: tomatoes, corn, basil, berries, cucumbers & beans.

Sep—Nov

Fall

Deep color & cozy harvests.

Try: squash, apples, kale, leeks, carrots & cider pears.

Dec—Feb

Winter

Sturdy roots & stored sunshine.

Try: potatoes, cabbage, chicories, onions, herbs & winter squash.
