A closer look

“Now I know
my voice counts.”

Marisol joined a learning circle last spring, hoping to feel more confident speaking English at work. She left with something bigger: a table of neighbors who now show up for one another.

“The first day, I only listened. By week six, I was helping lead. That changed how I see myself.”Marisol R. · learning circle participant

Today, Marisol co-facilitates a Saturday circle and helps new neighbors find the pantry, the garden, and the people who make both feel like home.
