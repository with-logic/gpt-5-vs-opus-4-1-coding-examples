Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment is included?

Who handles equipment maintenance?

We handle all maintenance, repairs, and regular servicing at no additional cost. If anything breaks or needs replacement, we'll swap it out immediately. You can also upgrade to newer equipment as it becomes available.

What does coaching include?

All plans include weekly live group workshops covering topics like extraction science, milk technique, latte art, and roasting profiles. You'll also get personalized 1-on-1 coaching sessions (frequency depends on your plan) and 24/7 access to our expert community on Slack.

Can I cancel anytime?

The monthly plan can be canceled anytime with 30 days notice. The 6-month and annual plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. After that, you're committed to the term, but we offer a satisfaction guarantee and will work with you if circumstances change.

Do I need any prior experience?

Not at all! We welcome everyone from complete beginners to experienced home baristas. Our curriculum is structured to meet you where you are and help you progress at your own pace.

What's the delivery and setup process?

We deliver all equipment directly to your home (free with 6-month+ plans, $99 for monthly). Our team will set everything up, calibrate it, and provide an initial training session. Setup typically takes 1-2 hours.
