Frequently Asked Questions

How does the CSA program work? +

When you join our CSA, you're purchasing a share of our farm's harvest. Each week or bi-weekly, you'll receive a box of fresh, seasonal produce picked at peak ripeness. The contents vary based on what's growing, giving you a true farm-to-table experience.

Can I customize my box? +

While we can't customize individual boxes, we offer add-ons like eggs, flowers, and bread that you can include with your delivery. We also provide recipe suggestions to help you make the most of each week's harvest.

What if I'm traveling or need to skip a week? +

No problem! You can pause your subscription or donate your box to a local food bank. Just let us know at least 48 hours before your scheduled delivery, and we'll take care of the rest.

Are your farming practices organic? +

Yes! We use organic and regenerative farming practices. While we're not certified organic, we never use synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. We focus on building healthy soil and supporting biodiversity on our farm.

How much does a typical box weigh? +

Our standard weekly box contains approximately 8-12 pounds of produce, featuring 6-9 different items depending on the season. This is typically enough for a family of 2-4 people for a week.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and ACH bank transfers. Payment is processed automatically based on your selected schedule (weekly or bi-weekly). You can update your payment method anytime in your account settings.
