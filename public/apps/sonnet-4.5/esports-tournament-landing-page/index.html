Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my team? +
Team registration opens January 15th. Visit our registration portal, complete the team roster form, and pay the entry fee ($500 per team). All team members must be 16+ with valid ID.
What are the ticket prices? +
General Admission: $50/day or $120 for 3-day pass. VIP: $200/day with backstage access, meet & greets, and premium seating. Student discounts available with valid ID.
Will the event be livestreamed? +
Yes! All matches will be livestreamed on Twitch and YouTube in 4K quality with professional commentary in multiple languages. VODs available immediately after each match.
What is the prize pool? +
$500,000 total prize pool. 1st place: $250,000 | 2nd place: $125,000 | 3rd-4th: $50,000 each | 5th-8th: $15,625 each. Plus championship trophies and exclusive in-game rewards.
Are there age restrictions? +
Players must be 16+ to compete. Spectators of all ages are welcome, but those under 13 must be accompanied by an adult. Valid ID required for entry.
