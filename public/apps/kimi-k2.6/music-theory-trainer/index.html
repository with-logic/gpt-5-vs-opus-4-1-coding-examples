How It Works

Intervals: Two notes will play sequentially. Identify the distance between them. Common intervals: Unison (same note), Major 2nd (whole step), Major 3rd (4 half-steps), Perfect 5th (7 half-steps), Octave (12 half-steps).

Chord Qualities: A chord will play. Identify whether it sounds Major (happy/bright), Minor (sad/dark), Diminished (tense/unstable), or Augmented (mysterious/floating).
