Questions, answered

If you do not see your question here, reach out anytime.

Easy. Log into your account before Thursday midnight and place a vacation hold. Your credits roll over automatically, or you can donate your box to a local food pantry with one click.

We offer a "never list" where you can exclude up to three items you simply will not eat. Beyond that, the beauty of a CSA is eating with the seasons. We promise you will discover new favorites.

Most items are harvested within 24 hours of delivery. Greens are picked at dawn, washed, cooled, and packed the same morning. Root vegetables may have been in storage briefly, but never longer than a week.

Yes. Choose the bi-weekly plan for a naturally smaller commitment, or select our "petite" box at checkout, which is about two-thirds the size of the standard share. Perfect for one to two people.

We use returnable, insulated totes for home delivery. Pickup members bring their own bags. Any necessary liners are compostable, and we are working toward zero-waste operations by 2026.

Absolutely. There is no contract. Pause for a season, switch frequencies, or cancel anytime before your next billing date. We will always refund a prorated balance if you paid ahead.
