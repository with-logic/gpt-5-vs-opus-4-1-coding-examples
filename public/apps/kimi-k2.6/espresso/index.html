The Problem

Great Coffee at Home Is Harder Than It Looks

You've tried the pour-over, the fancy grinder, the YouTube tutorials. But the gap between cafe-quality and home-quality espresso is measured in thousands of dollars and years of trial and error.

Equipment Is Prohibitively Expensive

A decent home setup starts at $2,000. A great one pushes $6,000+. Most enthusiasts buy the wrong gear, upgrade twice, and still end up disappointed.

Starter Setup: $2,500 — $6,000+

The Learning Curve Is Brutal

Dialling in espresso is a systems problem. Variables compound: grind size, dose, yield, temperature, pressure, roast profile, bean age. Without guidance, you're debugging blind.

Time to Competence: 12+ months

Fresh Green Beans Are Hard to Source

Roasting your own requires access to quality green coffee. Most home roasters buy from brokers with 50lb minimums, no provenance data, and questionable freshness.

Minimum Order: 50 lbs

How It Works

From Unboxing to Perfect Shot

We've designed the experience to get you from zero to confident in 30 days.

01

Onboarding & Setup

We deliver your starter kit and schedule a 90-minute in-home setup session. A coach walks you through machine calibration, grinder dialling, and your first roast. You'll pull your first decent shot before we leave.

02

Monthly Bean Box

Each month, receive 2 lbs of curated green coffee with roast profiles designed by our head roaster. Follow the guide, or use the Bullet's automated profiles as training wheels.

03

Video Coaching Sessions

Upload photos of your roasts and videos of your shots. Your coach reviews them live, diagnoses issues, and gives you a clear action plan for the next two weeks.

04

Iterate & Upgrade

Log every shot in our app. Watch your extraction yield trend up and your variance tighten. After 3 months, you're eligible to swap equipment and tackle advanced techniques.
