FAQ & rules

When do doors open?

Doors open at 10:00 local time each day. Premium floor entry starts 30 minutes earlier.

Can I transfer tickets?

Tickets can be transferred until 24 hours before the session through the ticket account page.

What is the tournament format?

Sixteen teams play a single-elimination bracket with best-of-three matches and a best-of-five final.
