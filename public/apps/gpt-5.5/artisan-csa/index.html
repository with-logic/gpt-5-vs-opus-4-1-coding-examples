FAQ + Contact

Good questions before harvest.

Need a pause, swap, pickup change, or pantry note? Send a message and a real person from the packing shed will reply.

Can I pause a week?
Yes. Pause by Sunday night and your credit rolls to a future delivery or a pantry add-on.
Do you offer substitutions?
Members can mark dislikes and allergies. We also set up a swap crate at each pickup while supplies last.
How is delivery packed?
Shares arrive in insulated, returnable totes with damp cloth wraps for tender greens and labeled pantry goods.
What happens during winter?
Winter shares move bi-weekly by default and lean into storage crops, greenhouse greens, citrus partners, grains, and soup staples.
