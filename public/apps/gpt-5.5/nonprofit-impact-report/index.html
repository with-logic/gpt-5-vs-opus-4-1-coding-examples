How support travels from donation to outcome.

Funds are pooled into practical interventions, delivered through local partners, and measured through follow-up milestones.

1. Local signals Partners surface urgent needs from schools, clinics, tenant groups, and food pantry visits.
2. Matched response Case teams assign the right mix of supplies, coaching, referrals, and direct assistance.
3. Delivery hubs Neighborhood sites reduce travel time and keep services close to participants.
4. Verified outcomes Milestones are checked at intake, completion, and 90 days after service.
