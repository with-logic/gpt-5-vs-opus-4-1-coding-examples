Notes

Every white or black key has a pitch name. Sharps move one half step higher; flats name the same key from the other direction.

Scales

A scale is a pattern of steps. Major sounds bright: whole, whole, half, whole, whole, whole, half.

Chords

Chords stack notes from a scale. A major triad uses root, major third, and perfect fifth.
