02 — Design Philosophy

We removed

ARC's display uses our PaperLight OLED — capable of 1 nit warm paper tone to 1600 nit outdoor. At rest, it mimics brushed titanium, not a black rectangle.

Machined from a single block of grade-5 titanium, each case spends 4 hours in a tumbler with ceramic stones, then vapor-blasted. No logos on the front. Only a micro-etched FORM on the lug. It weighs 38 grams. You will forget it's there until you need it — which is the point.
