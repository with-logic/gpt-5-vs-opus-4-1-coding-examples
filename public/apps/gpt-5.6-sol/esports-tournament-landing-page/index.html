Need to know

Frequently asked.

What does a ticket include?

Every pass includes arena entry, expo floor access, free fan activities, and digital viewer drops. Weekend passes also include priority entry.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes. Ticket transfers are available in your order portal until 18:00 PDT on July 23. Resale outside the official exchange is not protected.

Are under-18s allowed?

Yes. Guests under 16 must attend with an adult. Photo ID is required for age-restricted sponsor activations.

What is your bag policy?

Clear bags up to 12 × 6 × 12 inches are permitted. Medical and accessibility exceptions are screened at the South Entrance.
