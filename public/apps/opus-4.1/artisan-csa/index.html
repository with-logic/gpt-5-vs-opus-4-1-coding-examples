Frequently Asked Questions

CSA stands for Community Supported Agriculture. When you join our CSA, you're purchasing a "share" of our harvest. This means you receive a weekly or bi-weekly box of fresh, seasonal produce throughout the growing season. It's a partnership between you and our farm!

We send out a weekly newsletter every Monday with a preview of what's coming in that week's boxes, along with storage tips and recipes. The exact contents depend on what's ready for harvest, but we always ensure a good variety and generous portions.

While we can't customize individual boxes, we do offer a swap table at all pickup locations where members can trade items. We also note any allergies and try to provide alternatives when possible. Our add-on options let you supplement your box with eggs, flowers, and bread.

We understand life happens! You can pause your subscription for up to 2 weeks per season, or donate your box to our community food bank. Just give us a week's notice through your member portal or by contacting us directly.

Yes! While we're not certified organic (it's expensive for small farms), we follow organic practices: no synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, crop rotation, cover cropping, and integrated pest management. You're welcome to visit the farm and see our methods firsthand!
