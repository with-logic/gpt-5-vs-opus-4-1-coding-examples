No. Pre-ordering simply reserves your watch and locks in the $399 founder's price. Your payment method is only charged when your NOVA is ready to ship.

Does it work with my phone? +

Yes. NOVA OS 3.0 pairs fully with iOS 17 and later, and Android 12 and later — including notifications, calls, health sync, and contactless payments on both platforms.

Is the 14-day battery claim real? +

It's based on typical use: always-on display, all-day health tracking, and notifications. Heavy GPS workouts or LTE streaming will reduce that — but even then you'll comfortably clear a week.
