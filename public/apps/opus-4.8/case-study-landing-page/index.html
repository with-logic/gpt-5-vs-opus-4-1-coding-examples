The Challenge & The Fix

Hypergrowth broke their support model overnight

A 4× jump in customers exposed every crack in a manual, ticket-by-ticket process. Helm replaced the firefighting with a system that scales.

Before Helm

A support team in permanent triage

  • ✕14+ hour resolution times. Tickets piled up faster than agents could read them, and SLAs slipped daily.
  • ✕Knowledge trapped in people's heads. New hires took 6 weeks to ramp; answers lived in scattered Slack threads.
  • ✕No visibility into what was breaking. Leadership flew blind on root causes and recurring issues.
  • ✕Burnout and churn. Two senior agents quit in a quarter, citing the relentless backlog.
With Helm

An AI-assisted support engine

  • ✓Auto-triage & smart routing. Every ticket is classified, prioritized, and routed to the right person in seconds.
  • ✓A living knowledge base. Helm surfaces the exact answer from past resolutions, docs, and code — instantly.
  • ✓Real-time analytics. Leaders see trends, deflection rates, and emerging issues on one dashboard.
  • ✓Happier, focused agents. Repetitive work is automated so the team handles only what truly needs a human.
