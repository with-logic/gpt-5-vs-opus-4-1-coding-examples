Questions

Everything you're wondering.

Do I need any experience to join?+

None at all. About a third of our members start having never pulled a real shot. Your onboarding maps where you are, and your coach meets you there — whether that's "what's a portafilter" or "help me fix my roast development time."

What equipment can I actually take home?+

Espresso machines (La Marzocco, Profitec, ECM), grinders (Niche, Eureka), tabletop roasters (Aillio Bullet, Sandbox Smart), refractometers, precision scales, and more. You can hold up to two major pieces at a time and swap whenever you like, subject to availability.

How does the coaching work?+

Book unlimited 1:1 sessions through your member dashboard — in person at our SoMa lab or live over video at your own counter. Sessions run 45–60 minutes and are tailored to whatever you're working on that week.

Why commit to six months?+

Honestly? Because mastery takes more than a few weekends. Six months is roughly how long it takes to go from curious to genuinely skilled — and committing unlocks priority gear, a dedicated coach, and 30% off. You're protected by our 14-day guarantee, so there's no risk in starting.

Where are you located, and do you deliver?+

Our lab is in SoMa, San Francisco. Six-month members get free delivery and pickup across San Francisco, the Peninsula, and the East Bay. Monthly members are welcome to pick up at the lab.

What if I damage a machine?+

Accidental damage is covered by your membership — no deductible, no drama. Routine maintenance, descaling, and calibration are all handled by us, too. Just make great coffee.
