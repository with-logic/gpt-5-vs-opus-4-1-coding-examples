The Art &
Science of Espresso

You don't just drink coffee. You study it. You chase the perfect extraction curve. You taste terroir the way others taste wine. Most people think good espresso is luck — you know it's thermodynamics, particle size distribution, and a well-seasoned burr set.

Bay Area Espresso Lab exists for that mindset. We provide the equipment, the data, and the one-on-one coaching to turn your kitchen into a micro-roastery. Not a hobby. A discipline.

01

Roasting Mastery

Learn to read first and second crack. Understand development time ratios. Roast small batches with our commercial-grade drum roasters until you find your signature profile.

02

Espresso Engineering

Dial in grind size to the micron. Measure extraction yield and TDS. Learn how pressure profiling, temperature, and pre-infusion interact to produce syrupy, balanced shots.

03

Sensory Science

Train your palate with guided cupping sessions. Understand acidity, body, sweetness, and bitterness not as adjectives but as quantifiable variables in flavor architecture.
