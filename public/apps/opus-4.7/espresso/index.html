The Method

From green bean to golden crema
— in six months.

We approach coffee the way Bay Area engineers approach hard problems: with instrumentation, iteration, and relentless curiosity. Here's what your first six months look like.

01 / The Kit Arrives

The Lab Kit

A white-glove delivery: commercial-grade espresso machine, conical burr grinder, drum roaster, scales, refractometer, and a green bean selection curated for your palette.

02 / Calibration

Kickoff Session

A 90-minute 1:1 with your dedicated coach, either in-home (Peninsula & East Bay) or live remote. We dial in, taste, and chart your coffee goals.

Roasting Curriculum

Bi-weekly video labs and a private Slack with your cohort. Work through profiles, analyze your cup scores, and learn the thermodynamics of the Maillard reaction.

Questions

Before you commit.

What happens if something breaks?
Every piece of equipment is serviced by our SF Lab. We ship a replacement within 48 hours, swap during a home visit in the core Bay Area, and never charge for normal wear-and-tear. If you damage something outside normal use, we'll repair or replace at cost — there is no deductible.
Is this only for beginners — or advanced?
Both. Intake is a 20-minute sensory and skills assessment. Beginners start on the Foundations track (extraction science, milk texturing, your first roast). Advanced members go straight to flow profiling, competition-style roast development, and origin deep-dives.
Do I need to live in San Francisco?
We currently operate in SF, the Peninsula, East Bay, and Marin — anywhere we can send a same-day technician. Members in South Bay / Sonoma get service scheduled weekly. We do not currently ship outside the Bay.
Can I keep the equipment?
At the end of a 6-month residency, yes — at 40% off MSRP. Most members don't. They swap to a new kit (we cycle Slayer, Profitec, Decent machines through active members) and stay for a second cohort.
How is this different from a class at a roastery?
A class teaches you on their equipment, in their space, with twelve other students. The minute you're home, you're on your $400 machine with no one to ask. We put the pro tools in your kitchen every morning, with your coach a Slack away.
What's the early cancellation policy?
First 30 days: full refund, we pick up the kit. After 30 days on the 6-month plan, you'd pay out the remaining two months at the monthly rate ($200/mo). That's it. No pro-rated equipment fees, no restocking gotchas.
