The Story

From fragmented workflows to a single source of truth.

XYZ Corp's revenue operations were scaling, but their stack wasn't. Here's what changed.

⚠ The Problem

Three tools. Two spreadsheets. Zero clarity.

XYZ Corp's 60-person revenue team was juggling a CRM, a ticketing system, and a homegrown onboarding tracker. Data lived everywhere and nowhere — deals went dark, implementations slipped, and leadership was flying blind going into each QBR.

  • 14-day average onboarding stretched to 52 days in Q2
  • 31% of renewal conversations missed their target window
  • Two full-time analysts spent 60% of their week reconciling reports
  • Churn crept up to 18% — threatening their Series C narrative
✦ The Solution

Lumen unified their workflow layer in six weeks.

Rather than rip and replace, XYZ Corp deployed Lumen as an orchestration layer on top of their existing stack — syncing data bidirectionally, automating handoffs, and giving every rep a real-time view into the full customer lifecycle.

  • Unified dashboard across sales, CS, and implementation
  • Automated onboarding workflows with SLA enforcement
  • AI-powered churn signals surfaced 60 days in advance
  • Executive reporting that refreshes in real time, not weeks
Rollout

A six-week deployment, not a six-month project.

Lumen's integration engineers embedded with the XYZ Corp team to build, migrate, and train in record time.

Week 1
Discovery & data mapping
Mapped 127 data fields across three existing systems and drafted a shared schema with XYZ Corp's RevOps leads.
Week 2 – 3
Integration & automation build
Connected Salesforce, Zendesk, and the internal onboarding tracker. Built 22 automated workflows replacing 140+ manual steps.
Week 4
Pilot launch with CS team
Rolled out to 12 CSMs. Collected qualitative feedback and tuned churn-risk scoring to match XYZ Corp's customer segments.
Week 5
Company-wide training
Certified 60 team members through Lumen Academy. Ran live office hours with leadership to unblock adoption.
Week 6
Full go-live
Decommissioned the legacy tracker. Unified dashboard went live across sales, CS, and leadership the same day.

Lumen didn't just save us time — it gave us our narrative back. For the first time, every team at XYZ Corp is looking at the same picture of the customer, and our board can see the flywheel finally spinning.
