Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our harvest

Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) is a partnership between you and local farmers. You subscribe to receive a regular box of seasonal produce directly from our network of small artisan farms. Each week (or every other week), your box is filled with what's freshest and most abundant right now — no middlemen, no grocery store markup. You get to know your food, your farmers, and the land it comes from.
Currently, our boxes are curated by our farmers to reflect what's at peak harvest — this is part of the joy and adventure of seasonal eating! However, members can note one or two dislikes in their profile (e.g., "no cilantro"), and we'll substitute when possible. Harvest and Abundance members also receive early access to our seasonal swap marketplace.
No problem at all! You can pause, skip, or reschedule any delivery from your member dashboard with at least 48 hours notice. Skipped weeks can be credited toward future deliveries or donated to our food bank partner, Second Harvest. We never charge you for deliveries you skip ahead of time.
Seedling (Small): 6–8 items, perfect for 1–2 people or those new to CSA. Great for supplementing your regular grocery shopping.

Harvest (Medium): 10–12 items for 2–4 people. Our most popular box — enough variety to make 4–5 meals per week from mostly farm produce.

Abundance (Large): 14–16 items, ideal for families of 4–6 or enthusiastic cooks. Also includes monthly recipe booklets and an annual farm visit invitation.
We are deeply committed to sustainability. All our boxes are made from 100% recycled cardboard and are fully compostable. Produce is bundled with natural twine or paper bands — zero plastic wrap. For home deliveries, we use insulated wool liners (returned on next delivery) instead of foam. Our farms are all within 80 miles of the city, minimizing transportation emissions.
No long-term contracts! You can cancel anytime with one week's notice. Many traditional CSAs require upfront seasonal payments, but we bill weekly or bi-weekly to keep things flexible. We do offer a discounted rate for members who commit to a full season (12 weeks) — ask us about seasonal pricing when you sign up.
