APEX Unveils the Next Evolution in Personal Health Technology

San Francisco, CA — APEX Technologies today announced the Apex One, a groundbreaking smartwatch that sets a new standard for wearable performance, health monitoring, and intelligent design. Built on a custom 4nm neural processing chip, the Apex One delivers computational power previously reserved for smartphones, now worn on the wrist.

"We didn't build another smartwatch. We built the first personal health computer you can wear," said APEX CEO. "The Apex One knows your body better than any device ever has — tracking over 50 health biomarkers continuously, 24 hours a day."

With an industry-first 21-day adaptive battery life, a 2000-nit always-on neural display that adjusts to ambient conditions in under 1 millisecond, and a aerospace-grade titanium chassis rated to 200 meters of water resistance, the Apex One is engineered without compromise.

Pre-orders open today starting at $299 for the Standard Edition, with shipping expected Q3 2026. The Pro and Titanium editions will also be available.
