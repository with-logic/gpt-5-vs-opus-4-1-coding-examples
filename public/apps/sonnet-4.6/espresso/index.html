FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need any prior coffee experience?
+

Not at all. We've onboarded complete beginners and SCA-certified home baristas alike. Your coach will calibrate the program to exactly where you are. All that's required is curiosity and a kitchen counter.

What happens to the equipment after 6 months?
+

You return the equipment, or you can choose to purchase it at a significantly discounted alumni rate (typically 40% off retail). Many members continue with a maintenance-only subscription and purchase their setup outright.

How much counter space do I need?
+

The roaster needs roughly 18" × 16" of ventilated counter space plus access to a standard 110V outlet. The espresso machine needs about 12" × 16". We assess your space during sign-up and can advise on setup configurations.

Is there a smoke issue with home roasting?
+

The Aillio Bullet has a built-in afterburner that handles the vast majority of smoke. Roasting near an open window or with a range hood is sufficient for most apartments. Your coach will walk you through ventilation best practices during setup.

What if the equipment breaks or malfunctions?
+

We guarantee 48-hour swap or repair for any equipment malfunction. If a part needs to be ordered, we'll provide a loaner unit. You'll never be without your setup for more than two days.

Which areas of the Bay Area do you serve?
+

We currently deliver and service San Francisco, Marin, the East Bay (Oakland, Berkeley, Walnut Creek), and the South Bay (San Jose, Palo Alto, Cupertino, Sunnyvale). Expanding to the Peninsula in Q2 2026.
