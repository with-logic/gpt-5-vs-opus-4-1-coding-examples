The challenge & solution

Breaking down the before & after

XYZ Corp was growing fast — but their fragmented tech stack was siloing data, slowing deals, and eroding margins.

⚠️

The Problems They Faced

  • Sales, marketing, and CS teams operated in three separate CRMs with no shared visibility
  • Manual forecasting took 12+ hours per week and was wrong 40% of the time
  • Deal reviews relied on gut feel — no consistent methodology or pipeline hygiene
  • Onboarding new reps took 4–6 months, delaying quota attainment
  • No early-warning system for at-risk renewals; churn came as a surprise
✅

What SaaSCo Delivered

  • Single unified data layer connecting all revenue-facing teams in real time
  • AI-powered forecasting engine with 95%+ accuracy, updating automatically
  • Embedded deal coaching and MEDDIC scoring at every stage of the funnel
  • Dynamic onboarding paths cut ramp time to under 6 weeks for new AEs
  • Predictive health scores flagged at-risk accounts 90 days before renewal

How we got there

A phased rollout designed for speed

XYZ Corp went from signed contract to measurable impact in just 6 weeks — without disrupting their active quarter.

1
Week 1–2 · Discovery
Data audit & integration mapping
Our solutions engineers mapped XYZ Corp's existing data flows, identified gaps in their HubSpot, Salesforce, and Gainsight environments, and designed a unified integration architecture — all without access to production systems.
2
Week 3–4 · Integration
Zero-downtime data unification
Live connectors were deployed using read-only APIs. Historical data was backfilled 36 months, enriched with third-party intent signals, and validated against XYZ Corp's own reporting before going live.
3
Week 5–6 · Enablement
Team activation & change management
Role-based training tracks were delivered to 120 users across sales, CS, and RevOps. Custom playbooks were embedded directly in the workflow so reps never had to leave their existing tools.
4
Month 2–18 · Optimize
Continuous iteration & expansion
A dedicated Customer Success Manager ran monthly business reviews, identified new use cases, and helped XYZ Corp expand from the US Sales team to EMEA and APAC — growing from 120 to 340 seats over the engagement.
