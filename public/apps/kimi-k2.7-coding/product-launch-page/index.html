Nexus Wearables unveils Nexus Pulse, the smartwatch built for the next decade.

Nexus Wearables today introduced Nexus Pulse, a premium smartwatch that combines aerospace-grade titanium, a 1.8-inch always-on AMOLED display, and a comprehensive health sensor array in a design that is thinner, lighter, and more durable than anything in its class.

Starting at $399, Nexus Pulse is available for pre-order beginning today, with retail availability beginning October 2026. The watch will be offered in Midnight Titanium, Stellar Silver, and Solar Gold finishes.

  • 1.8" AMOLED display with 2000 nits peak brightness
  • Advanced health suite with ECG, SpO₂, and skin temperature
  • Up to 7 days of battery life with typical use
  • Precision dual-band GPS and 50-meter water resistance

"Nexus Pulse represents the biggest leap forward in wearable technology since the category began," said Maya Chen, CEO of Nexus Wearables. "We set out to build a watch that feels invisible on your wrist but indispensable in your life."

Feature set

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Nexus Pulse pairs cutting-edge sensor technology with aerospace-grade materials to deliver a premium wearable experience.

Always-On AMOLED

A 1.8" 466×466 display with 2000 nits peak brightness and an always-on mode that's easy to read in any light.

Advanced Health Suite

ECG, SpO₂, skin temperature, and continuous heart rate monitoring keep you informed around the clock.

7-Day Battery

Engineered for real life: up to 7 days of typical use, or 24 hours with always-on display active.

Dual-Band GPS

Precision tracking for running, cycling, and hiking with multi-frequency GNSS and offline maps.

50m Water Resistance

Swim, surf, and shower without worry. Nexus Pulse is rated for 50 meters of water resistance.

Titanium Design

Aerospace-grade titanium case with a sapphire crystal front and a weight of just 42 grams.
