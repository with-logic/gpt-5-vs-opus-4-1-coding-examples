How to use

  • Piano: Click the keys or use your keyboard (Z-M with S, D, G, H, J for sharps). See the detected chord update live.
  • Scales & Chords: Pick a root and a type. The notes are highlighted on the staff and piano, and you can hear them.
  • Ear Training: Press Play, listen carefully, then choose the correct interval or chord quality.
  • All sounds are generated in your browser; no external audio files are required.
