The Challenge

Growth was outpacing their infrastructure

XYZ Corp had built an incredible product, but rapid scaling exposed gaps in their support, analytics, and onboarding workflows. Their team was drowning in tickets, churn was climbing, and they needed a solution that could scale with them.

Fragmented support stack

Five disconnected tools meant agents had to switch contexts constantly, increasing response time and missed SLAs.

Reactive onboarding

Manual onboarding created bottlenecks, leading to long time-to-value and frustrated enterprise customers.

No unified analytics

Leadership lacked real-time visibility into churn signals, usage trends, and support health.

The Solution

A unified platform built for scale

We partnered with XYZ Corp to consolidate their support, onboarding, and analytics into a single integrated platform. The result: faster resolution, smarter automation, and crystal-clear visibility.

  • Single-pane support workspace

    Unified tickets, live chat, and knowledge base into one intelligent workspace.

  • Automated onboarding journeys

    Behavior-triggered checklists and in-app guidance cut time-to-value by 40%.

  • Predictive health scoring

    AI-powered churn signals enabled proactive success outreach before issues escalated.

  • Real-time executive dashboards

    Leadership now tracks usage, support load, and NRR in real time.
