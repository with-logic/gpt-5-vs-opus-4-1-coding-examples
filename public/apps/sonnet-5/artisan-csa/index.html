What's in the box

A share for every season

Every box is packed the same week it's picked. Hover or focus a card to see what's typically inside.

Spring

Spring Awakening

Tender greens and the first sweet roots of the year.

Typically includes Asparagus, spinach, baby radishes, snap peas, strawberries, new potatoes, spring onions.
Summer

Summer Abundance

Peak-season tomatoes, stone fruit, and sun-warmed berries.

Typically includes Heirloom tomatoes, sweet corn, zucchini, peaches, blueberries, cucumbers, basil.
Fall

Fall Harvest

Squash, apples, and hearty roots for cooler nights.

Typically includes Butternut squash, apples, beets, brussels sprouts, kale, cranberries, leeks.
Winter

Winter Roots

Stored roots, citrus, and warming greens for the slow months.

Typically includes Carrots, parsnips, citrus, winter squash, hardy greens, stored onions, garlic.
