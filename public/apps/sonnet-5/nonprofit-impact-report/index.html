Amara's Walk to School

At 11, Amara spent four hours a day walking for water instead of attending class. Since her village's well was completed in March, she's enrolled full-time and ranks first in her class.

Amara, age 12 — Kenema District

A Clinic Without Cholera

Nurse Fatima used to treat ten waterborne illness cases a week. After the community filtration system went live, that number dropped to less than one a month — freeing her clinic to focus on maternal care.

F
Fatima, Community Health Lead
