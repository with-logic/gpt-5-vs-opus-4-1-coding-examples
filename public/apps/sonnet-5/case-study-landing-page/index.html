The Challenge

Growth was outrunning the support team

XYZ Corp's customer base tripled in 18 months — but their support tooling, headcount, and onboarding playbooks were built for a company a third of the size.

⚠️

Where it hurt

  • ✕ First response times had crept past 14 hours during peak season, well outside SLA.
  • ✕ Onboarding new enterprise accounts took 6+ weeks and relied on tribal knowledge spread across spreadsheets.
  • ✕ Support headcount couldn't scale fast enough to match a 3x increase in ticket volume.
  • ✕ Leadership had zero real-time visibility into churn risk signals hiding inside support conversations.
✅

What changed

  • ✓ CloudPilot's AI triage layer now auto-classifies and routes 80% of inbound tickets instantly.
  • ✓ A guided, self-serve onboarding flow replaced manual spreadsheets, cutting setup from weeks to days.
  • ✓ Existing support staff now resolve 3x the ticket volume with the same headcount.
  • ✓ A live health-score dashboard surfaces churn risk to CS leadership in real time.
