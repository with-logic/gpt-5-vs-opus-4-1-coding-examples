Built like hardware. Framed like a statement.

This launch is about reducing compromise. Nova Pulse X combines advanced fitness instrumentation with a silhouette that feels closer to precision watchmaking than disposable consumer tech.

The enclosure is machined from recycled aerospace aluminum and finished with a ceramic backplate for reliable charging and skin-level comfort. The edge-to-edge AMOLED panel curves subtly into the chassis, pulling brightness and depth forward without sacrificing durability.

Inside, a new low-power architecture balances GPS, sleep analysis, heart-rate variability, skin temperature, and all-day notifications while keeping battery anxiety out of the experience. Whether you are training before sunrise or closing a week of travel, the device adapts its display and sensor load automatically.

PulseOS was designed around moments, not menus. Swipe to your body metrics, glance at readiness, route a call from the wrist, or start a run in seconds. Every interface is tuned for movement, outdoor light, and one-handed use.
