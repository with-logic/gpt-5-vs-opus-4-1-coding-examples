Program design

A tight six-month cadence designed for busy, detail-oriented people.

You do not need more content. You need a sequence that compounds. Each month adds one new variable, one new habit, and one new benchmark, so your setup gets better instead of more complicated.

01

Bench setup and taste mapping

We deliver your initial equipment package, map your current preferences, and establish a baseline extraction routine you can sustain on weekdays.

02

Dial-in discipline

You learn a repeatable recipe framework for dose, ratio, pressure, and shot evaluation, so adjustments become analytical instead of emotional.

03

Roast profiling at home

Once extraction stabilizes, we introduce roasting fundamentals, cupping notes, and controlled iterations that align your roast choices with your espresso goals.

04

Milk texture and signature drinks

For members who care about cappuccinos and cortados, we tune steam workflow, pitcher position, and integration so milk drinks stay precise, not sloppy.
