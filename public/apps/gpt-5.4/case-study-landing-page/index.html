Problem / Solution Breakdown

What was holding growth back and what changed.

The core issue was not product quality. It was the journey around the product. XYZ Corp aligned success, support, and sales around one shared implementation rhythm.

!
Before

The problem

  • Inconsistent launch experience Each new customer received a different onboarding path, making outcomes hard to predict and harder to scale.
  • Slow stakeholder alignment Admins were trained, but end users and executives lacked visibility into milestones, adoption goals, and expected value.
  • Reactive support model The team learned about risk only after tickets spiked or renewal conversations became difficult.
+
After

The solution

  • Milestone-based onboarding Every account moved through a standardized launch plan with success criteria tied to real customer workflows.
  • Role-specific enablement Champions, operators, and executive sponsors each received targeted communication and training content.
  • Health scoring and intervention Product usage, completion signals, and support patterns were surfaced early so teams could step in before risk compounded.
