FAQ

Practical details, packed plainly.

Yes. Two planned skips are included each season. You can redirect a paused share to a friend, donate it to the community pantry, or shift it to a pickup table with forty-eight hours' notice.

The produce share itself is packed without nuts, dairy, or gluten ingredients. Eggs arrive in a separate carton, bread is labeled as containing gluten, and flower bundles are marked for pollen sensitivity.

Every tote includes a compact field note with storage guidance, one flexible recipe idea, and a short list of farms represented in that week's box. Members also receive a Thursday email with prep tips.

We use cloth totes, fiber egg cartons, and paper sleeves. Leave your empty tote at the doorstep or return it at pickup the following week; our crew sanitizes and recirculates everything usable.
