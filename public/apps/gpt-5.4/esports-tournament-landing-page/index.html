FAQs & Rules

Event Essentials

What does each ticket include?

General admission includes arena access and fan festival entry. Premium adds early entry and collectible badge. VIP includes lounge access and post-show photo opportunity.

What is the equipment policy for teams?

Players may bring approved peripherals for stage play. Tournament PCs, displays, and standardized audio stack are supplied on site.

How are rule updates communicated?

Official notices are published on the event portal, mirrored in Discord, and read to teams through player operations before the next match block.
