The transformation

Problem to solution

Fragmented tooling was capping growth. CloudFlow became the connective tissue across sales, ops, and finance.

The challenge

XYZ Corp’s revenue engine was stuck in second gear. Teams worked hard—but systems worked against them.

  • Seven disconnected tools across sales, quoting, and customer success—zero single source of truth
  • Average sales cycle of 94 days with 40% of deals dying from quote friction alone
  • Manual reporting meant leadership saw last week’s numbers, not today’s reality
  • Onboarding a new AE took 11 weeks before they hit quota productivity

The CloudFlow solution

A unified revenue workspace that collapsed tool sprawl and put real-time intelligence in every rep’s hands.

  • Replaced seven apps with one platform—CRM, CPQ, forecasting, and customer health in a single pane
  • AI-assisted quoting cut proposal time from 3 days to 40 minutes while raising win rates
  • Live pipeline dashboards with anomaly alerts gave execs a truthful view, hourly
  • Guided workflows + playbooks cut AE ramp from 11 weeks to under 4
