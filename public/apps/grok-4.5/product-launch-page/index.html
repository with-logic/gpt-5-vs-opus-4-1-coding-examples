Designed without compromise

Health. Speed. Beauty.

Every millimeter of Aether One is tuned for athletic precision and everyday elegance.

Clinical heart engine

Multi-path PPG sensors deliver ECG-adjacent accuracy for continuous heart rate, HRV, and irregular rhythm alerts.

72-hour power vault

Adaptive power system lasts three full days. 15-minute charge unlocks 24 hours of mixed use.

On-wrist intelligence

Aether OS learns your patterns — equips proactive coaching, smarter sleep insights, and zero-noise notifications.

1.9″ MicroLED canvas

Always-on 326 ppi display with adaptive 1–120 Hz refresh. Peak 2000 nits — readable under desert sun.

Titanium lightness

Grade-5 titanium case and sapphire glass. Just 32 grams on the wrist — lighter than most wristbands.

Eight optical pathways, dual skin-temperature sensors, and a new bioimpedance electrode array work together to map recovery, stress, SpO₂, and respiratory rate — all without removing the watch.

  • Stress & recovery scores with AI coaching
