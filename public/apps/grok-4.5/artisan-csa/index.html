Questions & kindness

FAQ & get in touch

  • Email us your restrictions and we'll do our best to swap items. Major allergens are labeled on each week's packing list, and our packing shed is nut-aware but not certified free of cross-contact.

  • No synthetic pesticides or herbicides. We farm regeneratively with cover crops, beneficial insects, and rotational grazing — certified organic pathways under review.

  • Month-to-month members can cancel anytime with 7 days' notice. Seasonal prepaid shares are non-refundable after the second delivery, but you're welcome to gift remaining boxes.
