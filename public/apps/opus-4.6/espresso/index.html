The 6-Month Curriculum

A structured path from curious
to connoisseur

Each month builds on the last, combining theory, technique, and palate development.

Month 1
Foundations of Extraction
Grind size, dose, yield, and time ‚Äî the four variables that control every shot. You'll learn to taste the difference between under- and over-extraction and dial in consistently.
Month 2
Water Chemistry & Temperature
Your water is 98% of your espresso. Learn mineral composition, filtration, and how temperature profiling affects flavor compounds. You'll build a water recipe for your setup.
Month 3
Milk Science & Latte Art
Microfoam physics, steam pressure technique, and the art of the pour. From basic hearts to rosettas, with a deep dive into oat, almond, and alternative milks.
Month 4
Single Origin Deep Dive
Ethiopian naturals, Guatemalan washed, Kenyan AA ‚Äî explore how terroir, processing, and roast profiles create distinct flavor narratives. Develop your tasting vocabulary.
Month 5
Pressure Profiling & Flow Control
Advanced techniques using the Decent DE1. Design custom pressure profiles, analyze shot data in real-time, and learn why blooming, ramping, and declining pressures unlock new flavors.
Month 6
Home Roasting & Independence
Roast your own beans with our included sample roaster. Learn first crack, development time, and how to roast to highlight specific flavor notes. Graduate with the skills to be fully self-sufficient.
