FAQs

What game is being played?
NEXUS Champions features the top 16 teams competing in VALORANT, the premier tactical FPS by Riot Games.
Are tickets refundable?
Tickets are refundable up to 14 days before the event. After that, exchanges are available for different days/seating.
Can I bring my own food?
Outside food and beverages are not permitted. The venue offers a wide variety of food and drink options including vegetarian and allergen-friendly choices.
Is there an age restriction?
All ages are welcome. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. VIP lounges are 18+ only.
Will there be meet & greets?
Yes! VIP ticket holders get exclusive meet & greet sessions with pro players and talent. Schedule will be announced 1 week before the event.
