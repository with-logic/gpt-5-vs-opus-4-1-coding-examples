Diagnosis & Prescription

The Challenge & Our Approach

Understanding the root causes allowed us to craft a targeted, high-impact solution.

The Problem

XYZ Corp was trapped in a cycle of technical debt and manual processes that were crippling their ability to scale and compete effectively.

  • Legacy infrastructure required 3+ weeks for each deployment cycle, causing missed market windows
  • Siloed data systems meant teams operated with conflicting metrics and outdated information
  • Manual customer onboarding took 14 days on average, driving 32% prospect abandonment
  • No real-time visibility into product usage, churn risk, or revenue forecasting

The Solution

We implemented a comprehensive platform modernization strategy, focusing on automation, unified analytics, and customer experience.

  • Cloud-native CI/CD pipeline enabling automated testing, staged rollouts, and sub-2-hour deployments
  • Unified data platform consolidating 12 data sources into a single real-time analytics dashboard
  • AI-powered onboarding workflow reducing customer activation from 14 days to under 48 hours
  • Predictive analytics engine for proactive churn prevention and expansion revenue identification
