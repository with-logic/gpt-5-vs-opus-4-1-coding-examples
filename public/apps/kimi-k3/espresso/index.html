The Curriculum

Six months. Zero to signature roast.

A structured curriculum designed by competition baristas and a food scientist. This is why the 6-month journey exists — transformation takes reps.

01Month

Dial In

Grinder calibration, puck prep, distribution, and the extraction framework. You will pull your first genuinely great shot this month — we guarantee it.

Milestone · First 18→36g in 28s
02Month

Milk & Texture

The physics of microfoam: stretch, roll, temperature windows. Latte art foundations — hearts, then tulips, then whatever your ego demands.

Milestone · Glossy paint-texture milk
03Month

Fire & First Crack

Your sample roaster arrives. Thermodynamics of the drum, charge temps, and your first batch — cupped blind against a local roaster's.

Milestone · First roast, first cupping
04Month

Profile Craft

Rate-of-rise curves, development ratios, and airflow. Learn to push acidity or sweetness with intent — then espresso-roast without scorching.

Milestone · A repeatable profile
05Month

Your Signature Program

Build your house espresso: blend design, post-roast blending, rest windows. Name it. Print a label. This is your coffee now.
