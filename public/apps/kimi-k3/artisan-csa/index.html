Questions & hello

Pause any week from your member page by Monday night. We’ll skip your box and your card won’t be charged — the carrots will wait patiently.

Yes. Every Sunday we post the week’s sketch of a box, and you can trade up to three items from the swap table. Beets for beans, no judgment.

Two of our four farms are certified; all four farm beyond-organic — no synthetic sprays, cover-cropped soil, very happy worms. We list each farm’s practices on the back of every box note.

Boxes leave the barn Thursday at 2pm. You’ll get a text with a two-hour window, and a photo when it lands. Prefer pickup? Choose any hub at checkout — it’s $4 less per box.

There isn’t one, really. Cancel whenever with two days’ notice. Seasonal commitments get a prorated refund and a handshake (optional but encouraged).
