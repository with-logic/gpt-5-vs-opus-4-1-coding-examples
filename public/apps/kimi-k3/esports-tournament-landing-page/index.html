FAQs

Can I get a refund on tickets?

Full refunds until July 31, 2026. After that, tickets are transferable for free through your account portal up to 24h before doors.

Is there an age limit?

All ages are welcome. Attendees under 14 must be accompanied by an adult. Ear protection is recommended for under-8s and is available free at guest services.

Can I bring cosplay props?

Yes — props must pass a peace-bonding check at entry. No metal blades, projectiles, or items over 1.2m. Cosplay repair station is on Level 1.

Is re-entry allowed?

Same-day re-entry is unlimited with your wristband. Keep it on — the arena floor is wristband-only after 6 PM.

Where do teams park?

General parking is in Garage C ($15/day). Rideshare drop-off is at the Neon Blvd north gate, 60m from the main entrance.
