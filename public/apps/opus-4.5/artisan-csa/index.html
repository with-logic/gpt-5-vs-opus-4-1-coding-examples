Frequently Asked Questions

When you subscribe to our CSA, you're purchasing a "share" of our farm's harvest. Each week (or bi-weekly, depending on your plan), you'll receive a box of freshly harvested, seasonal produce. The contents vary based on what's ripe and ready, ensuring you always get the freshest items possible.
While the core contents are determined by what's in season, we offer flexibility through our swap system. You can indicate preferences and allergies in your profile, and swap out items you don't want for alternatives when available.
No problem! You can pause your subscription with at least 48 hours notice. Simply log into your account and select the weeks you'd like to skip. You can also donate your box to a local food bank if you prefer.
All our partner farms follow organic and sustainable practices. Many are certified organic, while others follow organic methods but haven't pursued certification. We personally visit every farm to ensure they meet our high standards for quality and environmental responsibility.
