Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our farm family.

When you subscribe, you'll receive a box of fresh, seasonal produce delivered to your door or available for pickup at one of our locations. You can choose weekly or bi-weekly delivery, pause anytime, and customize with add-ons. Each box is packed the morning of delivery to ensure maximum freshness.
While the main box contents are determined by what's freshest and in-season, you can set permanent preferences for items you'd like to avoid (like cilantro haters, we see you!). You can also add extras like eggs, bread, flowers, and honey to any delivery.
No problem! Our boxes are packed with ice packs during warm months and can safely stay outside for up to 8 hours. You can also leave delivery instructions for a specific location, or switch to pickup at any of our partner locations if that works better for your schedule.
Yes! We're certified organic and have been farming without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers for over 15 years. We also practice regenerative agriculture, focusing on soil health, biodiversity, and carbon sequestration. You can visit the farm anytime to see our practices firsthand.
Life happens! You can pause your subscription for up to 8 weeks at a time through your online account, or cancel anytime with no fees or penalties. Just make changes by Wednesday for the following week's delivery. We also offer vacation holds for longer breaks.
