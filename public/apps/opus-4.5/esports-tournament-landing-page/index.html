FAQs

+ What games are featured?
The tournament features top competitive titles including tactical shooters, fighting games, and MOBAs.
+ Are tickets refundable?
Tickets are refundable up to 7 days before the event. After that, exchanges are available.
+ Is there an age limit?
The event is all ages. Those under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.
+ Can I bring my own equipment?
Registered teams may bring their own peripherals. Spectators may not bring gaming equipment.
