The Challenge

XYZ Corp was facing a critical bottleneck. As a rapidly expanding logistics giant, their legacy systems relied heavily on manual data entry and disjointed spreadsheets to track shipments across three continents.

This fragmentation led to:

  • Data Silos: Information was trapped in departmental bubbles, causing 12-hour delays in reporting.
  • High Error Rates: Manual entry resulted in a 4.5% error rate, costing the company millions in rectified shipments annually.
  • Scalability Cap: The team physically couldn't process more than 500 orders per hour without hiring more staff, limiting growth during peak seasons.

The Solution

Recognizing the need for a unified digital backbone, XYZ Corp partnered with ScaleFlow. The implementation focused on automating the "Order-to-Ship" lifecycle using ScaleFlow's API-first platform.

Automated Workflows

Replaced manual data entry with event-driven triggers, instantly routing orders to the correct warehouse.

Unified Dashboard

A single view for logistics managers to track global inventory in real-time, reducing meeting times by 50%.

Predictive Routing

AI-driven suggestions for the fastest shipping routes based on weather and traffic data.

Seamless Integration

Connected with existing ERPs (SAP and Oracle) in under 2 weeks with zero downtime.
