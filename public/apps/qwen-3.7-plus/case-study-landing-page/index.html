01 · The Problem

A support stack that couldn't keep up with growth.

By mid-2025, XYZ Corp had outgrown its own infrastructure. Three separate ticketing systems, a homegrown knowledge base, and a dozen Slack channels meant that no single team had a complete picture of the customer.

Enterprise renewals were slipping. Churn in the mid-market was creeping up. And the support team — already working around the clock — was burning out.

Fragmented data

Customer context lived in 7+ tools. Agents spent 11 minutes per ticket just finding information.

Slow response

Median first response was 9 hours. Enterprise SLAs were missed 34% of the time.

Team burnout

Agent turnover hit 41% annually. Onboarding a new hire took 8 weeks.

02 · The Solution

One platform. One customer record. Every team, aligned.

XYZ Corp deployed Atlas in three phases over nine months — unifying support, success, and account management on a single customer graph. The result wasn't just efficiency. It was a fundamentally different way of working with customers.
