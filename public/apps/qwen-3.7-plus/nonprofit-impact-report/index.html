Our Journey

A decade of milestones.

From a small team of five to a global movement — every step has been powered by people like you.

2015

The first spark

Founded with 5 volunteers and $12,000 in seed funding. First scholarship awarded in rural Ghana.

2018

1,000 students milestone

Expanded to 12 countries. Launched mobile health clinics serving 30+ rural communities.

2021

Pandemic response

Deployed $1.2M in emergency relief. Delivered 400,000 meals and 80,000 PPE kits across 3 continents.

2025

Record-breaking year

Reached 48,250 lives. Launched clean water initiative. Named Top 10 NGO by Global Impact Index.
