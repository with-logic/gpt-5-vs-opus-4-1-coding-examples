Your 6-Month Journey
From first pour to
perfect extraction.

A structured path that builds your skills progressively — each month designed to deepen your understanding and refine your craft.

M1-2

Foundations

Equipment setup and calibration. Learn extraction theory, dial in your grinder, and master the basics of espresso preparation. Understand the science behind water temperature, pressure, and brew ratios.

Equipment delivered & installed
M3-4

Roast Development

Begin roasting your own beans. Learn to read first and second crack, develop roast profiles, and understand how origin and processing affect flavor. Start connecting roast decisions to cup quality.

First custom roast profile
M5

Sensory & Refinement

Develop your palate through structured cupping sessions. Learn to identify defects, score coffee using SCA protocols, and make data-driven adjustments to your roast and brew parameters.

SCA cupping fundamentals
M6

Mastery & Signature

Create your signature blend and roast profile. Master milk texturing and basic latte art. Present your final project to your coach and receive your Espresso Lab certification.

Certification & signature blend
