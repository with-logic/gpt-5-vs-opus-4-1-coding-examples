Questions & Contact

We're Here to Help

When you join our CSA, you purchase a share of the farm's harvest in advance. Each delivery period, you receive a box of freshly harvested, seasonal produce. It's a direct partnership between you and the farm -- you share in the bounty of the seasons!

Absolutely! We understand life happens. You can pause your subscription for up to 4 weeks per season, or skip individual deliveries through your member portal. Just let us know by Tuesday for the following week's box.

We include recipe cards and storage tips for every item! If there's something you truly don't enjoy, let us know and we'll try to swap it in future boxes. We also have a sharing board at pickup locations where members can swap items they don't need.

We practice organic and regenerative farming methods, though we're currently in the process of obtaining our USDA Organic certification. We never use synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, and our soil health practices go beyond organic standards.

No contracts, no commitments! You can cancel anytime with no penalty. We do ask for 2 weeks notice before your next delivery. If you cancel mid-season, we'll refund the remaining unused portion of your share.

Yes! Each box includes allergen information. If you have specific allergies, we can customize your box to exclude certain items. Just note your allergies when signing up, and our farm team will ensure your box is safe and enjoyable.
