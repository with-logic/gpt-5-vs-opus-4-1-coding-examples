FAQs

How do I register a team? +
Visit our registration portal and submit your team roster (5 players + 1 substitute). Registration closes July 31, 2026. All players must be 16+ and have an active competitive account.
Can I get a refund on tickets? +
Full refunds available up to 30 days before the event. 50% refund up to 7 days before. No refunds within 7 days of the event. Contact support@nexusarena.gg.
Is the venue accessible? +
Yes! Tokyo Dome City Hall is fully wheelchair accessible with dedicated seating, hearing loops, sign language interpretation, and a quiet room. Contact us for specific needs.
What's the format? +
Group stage (Bo3 round-robin) → Single elimination bracket (Bo5 QFs/SFs, Bo7 Grand Final). All matches played on the official competition map pool.
