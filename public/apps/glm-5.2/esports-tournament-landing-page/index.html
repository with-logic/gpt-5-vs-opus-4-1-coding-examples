FAQ & Contact
When do gates open?

Doors open 90 minutes before the first match each day. Grand Final day opens 2 hours early for fan zone activities.

Is re-entry allowed?

Yes — same-day re-entry is permitted with your wristband at any gate.

Can I bring a camera?

Phones and compact cameras welcome. No detachable lenses, tripods, or streaming rigs.
