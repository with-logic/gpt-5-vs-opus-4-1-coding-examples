Our journey

Sixteen years, one through-line.

2009
A single well
Brightwater begins with one solar well in a Kenyan village, funded by a $4,000 community bake sale.
2013
First school partnership
Education program launches; 300 children enrolled across 2 community schools.
2017
Food Security initiative
Drought response expands into a permanent climate-resilient agriculture program.
2021
Crossed 500K lives
Cumulative impact surpasses half a million people across 12 regions.
2025
124,000 lives this year alone
23 regions, 1,400+ volunteers, and 87¢ of every dollar reaching the field — our most impactful year yet.
