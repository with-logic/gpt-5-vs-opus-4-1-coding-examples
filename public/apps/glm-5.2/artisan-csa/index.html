good to know

Questions & a hello.

Can’t find your answer? Drop us a line — a real human (often in muddy boots) writes back.

Community Supported Agriculture. You commit to a season of shares up front, which funds our seeds and labor — and in return you get a weekly box of whatever’s ripest. You eat with the farm, not the supermarket.

Yes — pause any week with two days’ notice, no questions. You can also double-up the week you get back, or donate your box to the neighborhood pantry.

Swap it. Every box comes with a swap list — trade any item you don’t love for extra of something you do, right at the pickup stop.

We follow organic practices but aren’t certified — the paperwork costs more than the tractor. We’re happy to walk the rows with you anytime.

Each season card lists allergen notes (pollen-heavy weeks, corn, citrus, nut-adjacent add-ons). Tell us your allergies at signup and we’ll flag any risky weeks in advance.
