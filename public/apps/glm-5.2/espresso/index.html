The Science

Coffee is a system you can measure.

We treat your kitchen like a lab bench. These are the numbers you'll live by for six months.

1:2Brew Ratio

18g in, 36g out — the espresso ratio you'll master before you ever break it on purpose.

18–22%Extraction Yield

The SCA window. Below 18% is sour & hollow; above 22% is bitter & astringent. Your refractometer finds it.

9 barPuck Pressure

The industry-standard extraction pressure your machine holds — and the variable you'll learn to never blame first.

12–14%Development Time

Share of roast after first crack. The single biggest lever on whether your bean tastes roasted or baked.

Before you commit

The questions everyone asks.

Why a six-month commitment?
Because there's no shortcut to palate calibration and roast intuition. We compress what takes hobbyists years into 26 guided weeks, but we can't compress it further. The curriculum is paced to give each phase time to rest, taste, and consolidate. A monthly hop-on-hop-off model would give you gear without the arc — and that's exactly the problem we exist to fix.
What if I already own a grinder or machine?
Great — you can use ours alongside yours for comparison, or we'll credit $40/month off your rate for any major piece you opt out of (machine, grinder, or roaster; one credit per membership). Most members keep the full bench so the coach is working from known, calibrated equipment.
How much counter space do I need?
Roughly 4 linear feet: the roaster lives in a pantry or garage and only comes out for roast day (it vents through a window adapter), while the machine, grinder, and scale occupy a permanent espresso station. We send a setup guide and your coach helps you plan the layout in week one.
What happens to the gear after six months?
At month six you have three options: continue month-to-month at $200 and keep the bench; return the gear and graduate to alumni status (monthly green-bean drops + group labs for $60/mo); or purchase your bench at a member-discounted price. There's no auto-renewal trap — you choose at graduation.
Is the coaching really one-on-one?
Yes. One 30-minute private video session per week with your assigned coach, plus the twice-monthly group labs with your 24-person cohort. You keep the same coach for the full six months so they learn your palate and your log.
I'm in the South Bay / Peninsula — is this only for SF?
It's everywhere the gear ships. We deliver and service across the entire Bay Area — SF, Peninsula, South Bay, East Bay, North Bay — and the coaching is remote, so all you need is a kitchen, a window for the roaster, and a camera for your sessions.
