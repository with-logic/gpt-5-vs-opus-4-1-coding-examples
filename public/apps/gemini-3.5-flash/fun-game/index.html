Pro-Tips to Survive

  • Keep an eye on the 🍊 Yuzu Coins! Grab them to spend on costumes in the shop.
  • Collecting consecutive Yuzus builds a Score Multiplier (up to 5x). Dodging alone won't get you on the leaderboard!
  • Some flying obstacles can only be slid under, while giant cheese rolling wheels must be hopped.
  • As your score grows, the game speed ramps up and the environment transitions from sunset hills to cosmic deep space!
