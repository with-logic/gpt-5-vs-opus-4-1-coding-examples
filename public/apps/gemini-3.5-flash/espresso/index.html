THE CURRICULUM

A structured path to sensory mastery.

Access isn't enough without execution. Each month focuses on dialing in a single domain of roasting and espresso physics with deep 1-on-1 expert coaching.

Month 1: Agricultural Physics & Green Sorting

WEEKS 1 - 4

Understand the biological roots of espresso flavor. Learn to assess green bean density, screen sizes, moisture levels, and detect defect structures. Your coach will guide you through your first unroasted microlot shipment from Colombia and Kenya, preparing your first custom drying curves.

Month 2: Thermodynamic Drum Dynamics & Drying Profiles

WEEKS 5 - 8

Master heat transfer dynamics in the Aillio Bullet R1 drum. Control conductional and convectional energy phases. Avoid physical defects like tipping and scorching, while learning how charge temperatures dictate roast profile shapes.

Month 3: Caramelization, First Crack & DTR Optimization

WEEKS 9 - 12

Dial in the Maillard and caramelization stages. Control the Rate of Rise (RoR) to slide into the crucial "Development Time Ratio" (DTR) sweet spot. Log thermal events as chemical cracks occur to target custom origin-specific parameters.

Month 4: Particle Size Distribution & Extraction Science

WEEKS 13 - 16

Transition to your Mahlkönig grinder and La Marzocco Linea machine. Study the physical kinetics of grind distribution (fines vs. boulders), learn portafilter puck preparation methods to neutralize fluid channel paths, and run volumetric profiling.

Month 5: Advanced Pressure & Flow Profiling

WEEKS 17 - 20

Manipulate flow-rate variables manually. Run slow, low-flow pre-infusion profiles to soften the puck structure before locking in at full 9-bar pressures, mimicking world-class competition extractions. Use custom refractometers to read exact Total Dissolved Solids (TDS).

Month 6: Custom Water Chemistry & Sensory Certification

WEEKS 21 - 24

Learn how calcium, magnesium, and buffer levels in water completely alter organic extraction solubility. Build tailored water recipes at home, culminating in a graduation sensory test judged by our expert panel of roasting judges.
