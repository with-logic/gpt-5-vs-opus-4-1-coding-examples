Dynamic Presentation Directives

Customize specific slides using HTML comments at the top of any slide section:

Slide Divider ---

Place --- on its own blank line to end the current slide and open a brand new slide.

Multi-Column Break +++

Write +++ on its own blank line to separate content into equal columns horizontally (Flex Columns).

Background & Text Overrides <!-- bg: #ff0055 -->

Change background/color variables dynamically: <!-- bg: linear-gradient(...) --> or <!-- color: #fff -->.

Custom Layout Selector <!-- layout: title | center -->

Toggle vertical and horizontal alignment: title creates huge centered titles, while center aligns standard items dynamically.

Speaker notes Note: Speaker commentary

Write Note: on its own line followed by content. It parses and appears exclusively in the Presenter View Dashboard telemetry screen.
