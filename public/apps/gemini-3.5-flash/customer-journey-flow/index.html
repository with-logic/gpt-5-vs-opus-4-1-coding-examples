Workspace Controls

Canvas Navigation

Press and drag on any empty space of the grid canvas to Pan / Scroll around. Use your mouse scrollwheel to Zoom in and out.

Adding & Arranging Steps

Click on any touchpoint node category from the left toolbox to spawn a node on the canvas. Drag nodes vertically to order them, or drag nodes horizontally across columns to transfer them to a different journey stage!

Connecting Touchpoints

Click and drag from the blue dot (Output Port) on the right edge of a source node, and drop it close to the left edge of any destination node to draw a flowing curve connection.

Running Simulations

Click "Run Simulation" in the navbar. Animated glowing customer tokens will travel along active paths based on calculated conversion drop-offs. Change conversion rates or initial volumes to see adjustments in real-time!
