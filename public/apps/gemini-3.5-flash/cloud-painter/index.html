How to Paint Sky!

  • 🖌️
    Draw Floating Clouds: Move your finger or mouse across the sky. Your lines become live drifting clouds!
  • ✈️
    Control Airplanes: Click a plane to make it loop-de-loop! Click and hold to fly it around yourself. The planes write soft vapor trails into the sky!
  • ☀️
    Play with Sun & Moon: Tap the happy Sun or crescent Moon in the corner to make them giggle, spin, or wink!
  • 🌧️
    Climate Weather: Turn on Rain or Snow to watch snowflakes float down and accumulate nicely on the ground!
  • 📸
    Save Masterpiece: Hit "Save Sky" to flash the camera and save your cloud artwork to show your friends!
