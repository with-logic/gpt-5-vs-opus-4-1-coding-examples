How to Play

Click targets before they shrink and fade away. Red dots near the center of a target score a Bullseye for extra points!

Standard Target (+100 pts)

Normal size and average duration. Bread and butter of points.

Golden Target (+300 pts)

Super fast and small, but yields massive scores. Pop immediately!

Freeze Target (+150 pts & Slow-mo)

Slows down time and spawning speed for 3s, making hits easier!

Bomb Target (-200 pts & Penalty)

DO NOT CLICK. Breaks your streak and costs 2 seconds of time!

Streak Multiplier! Click consecutive targets without missing or hitting bombs to increase your combo streak. A high combo multiplies all score payouts!
