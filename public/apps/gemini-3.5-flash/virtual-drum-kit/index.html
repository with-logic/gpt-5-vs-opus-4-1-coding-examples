💡 Professional Producer Tips:

  • Click on any drum or MPC pad to make it the Selected Instrument, opening up its custom synthesizer knobs!
  • Enable Live Grid Rec. When the loop is running, any taps you record will automatically lock onto the sequencer step matrix!
  • Use Swing to add classic groove / swing offset (shuffles the timing of offbeats).
  • Use the Master WAV Recorder to export pristine 44.1kHz audio files of your custom live jams and programmed sequences!
