💡 Pro Backpacker Packing Guidelines

  • Consumables vs. Base Weight: Keep your Base Weight (total weight minus food/fuel/water) below 10 lbs (4.5 kg) to achieve "Ultralight" status. This increases hiking endurance and reduces joint fatigue!
  • Sleeping bag goes first: Pack your puffy insulation and sleeping bag right at the bottom of the main compartment. This serves as a solid shock absorber and doesn't require access during the day.
  • Keep heavy items centered: Secure stove, pots, gas, and food supplies close to your spine, halfway up the pack. This keeps your center of gravity centered.
  • Access items up top: Place maps, headlamp, multi-tool, sanitizers, and rain jackets in the brain/lid pockets for instant reach.
