FAQ

Is there a deposit?
Yes, a fully refundable equipment deposit is assessed during onboarding based on your setup. It’s returned at the end of your membership when gear is picked up in good condition.
What if something breaks?
Normal wear is on us. If something fails, we repair or swap quickly. We also handle preventive maintenance and water filtration.
Where do you operate?
San Francisco, Peninsula, South Bay, East Bay, and North Bay. Some remote coaching is available outside the Bay Area—ask during the fit call.
Can I pause or cancel?
After your six‑month term, you can pause or cancel with no fees. During the term, reach out—we’ll work with you on solutions.
Do I keep the coffee?
Consumables like green coffee and water filters are separate, so you can chase the origins you love. We’ll recommend monthly bundles based on your taste.
Who is this best for?
Curious, detail‑oriented people who value both science and ritual—often folks in tech, biotech, and design who want a craft that rewards iteration.
