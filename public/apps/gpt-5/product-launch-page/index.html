SAN FRANCISCO — January 28, 2026

Lumen unveils Lumen S9, a smartwatch that puts time back in your day

Today Lumen introduced Lumen S9, a radical rethink of the smartwatch that balances performance and peace. With a micro‑LED display that’s visible in any light, an adaptive battery system that lasts up to three days, and our strongest titanium chassis yet, S9 is engineered for the unpredictable.

Inside, the new BioCore X2 focuses on the signals that matter, enabling high‑resolution heart metrics, SpO₂, skin temperature, and on‑device AI that surfaces insights without sending raw health data to the cloud. Combined with dual‑band GNSS and UWB, S9 keeps you oriented — from city blocks to backcountry switchbacks.

“We designed S9 to be the quietest computer you own,” said Mira Chen, VP of Product at Lumen. “It gets out of the way, lasts longer, and shows you just what you need when you need it.”
