Six months, designed like a product roadmap

Each month has one primary focus: learn the concept, run a small experiment, and lock in your “default” settings. You’ll get faster at dialing in, and better at tasting.

Cohort pace • no overwhelm

Baseline & workflow

Dial‑in mechanics

Design a house profile, roast to taste, and host a “café night” for friends or family.
