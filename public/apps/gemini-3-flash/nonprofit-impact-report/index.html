Success Story

Maria's Sustainable Grove

Maria was one of the first to join our Green Farming program in the Amazon basin. In just three years, she transformed her struggling coffee farm into a thriving agroforestry system that mimics the natural forest.

Today, she grows organic cacao, bananas, and native timber, providing habitat for local bird species while doubling her family's annual income.
