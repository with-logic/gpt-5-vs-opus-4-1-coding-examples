Problem & Solution

From fragmented workflows to a single, proactive command center.

Before: A high-growth team flying blind

XYZ Corp had no shortage of customers or data—but every team saw a different truth. The result was reactive firefighting instead of predictable growth.

Disparate systems Manual spreadsheets Lagging health signals Opaque renewals
  • Fragmented account data Usage, contracts, tickets, and billing lived in separate systems, forcing CSMs to stitch together insights before every customer touchpoint.
  • Reactive churn management Health scores were updated monthly in static decks. By the time risk surfaced, renewal conversations were already behind.
  • No shared view of expansion Sales, CS, and Finance tracked expansion opportunities in different tools, making it impossible to forecast net retention with confidence.

After: A single SaaS command center

We deployed a unified SaaS platform that consolidates signals, automates playbooks, and gives leadership a real-time, CFO-grade view of customer health.
