Product Narrative

Built for real days, not lab demos.

Nebula X1 is the first smartwatch designed around your actual week — long days, red‑eye flights, and the recovery you forget to plan.

A press‑ready wearable headline

Nebula X1 debuts a custom co‑processor that intelligently shifts between performance and deep‑sleep states, delivering up to seven days of battery life with 24/7 health tracking. The result is a watch that doesn’t ask you to compromise between bright visuals, accurate data, and genuine all‑week wear.

  • Launch colors: Midnight, Aurora, and Solar Edition
  • Available in 40 mm and 44 mm, 29 g starting weight
  • Press review units ship with Nebula OS 4.1 beta
