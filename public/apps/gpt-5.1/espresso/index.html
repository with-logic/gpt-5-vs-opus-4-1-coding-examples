The program

Six months from “good shots” to repeatable excellence.

Bay Area Espresso Lab is designed for people who think in systems: you’ll build a workflow, measure outcomes, and get targeted feedback over a focused six‑month arc.

01

Design your setup with a coach.

We start with a 45‑minute onboarding call to calibrate around your taste preferences, space constraints, and prior gear. You leave with a personalized espresso & roasting plan.

Includes water profile guidance, grinder recommendations, and a suggested schedule that respects your work and family life.

02

We deliver & install pro‑grade gear.

A temperature‑stable espresso machine, serious grinder, and a compact sample roaster arrive at your door. We help you set everything up over a live video walkthrough.

You get loaner equipment for the full 6 months with white‑glove support if anything misbehaves.

03

Roast, pull, measure & iterate.

Every week you’ll log a few shots and roasts, share video or data with your coach, and get concrete adjustments to try. Think of it as a combination of espresso standup and lab notebook.

By month six, you’ll have house recipes dialed in and a repeatable process you can run half‑awake at 6:30am.
