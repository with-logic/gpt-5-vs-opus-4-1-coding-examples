Course syllabus

A 12‑week syllabus designed to ship real projects.

Here’s the outline for our flagship Full‑Stack Product Builder course. Every track follows the same rhythm of foundations, projects, and launch.

  1. Weeks 1–2 · Foundations

    Guided labs

    Modern web fundamentals & learning toolkit

    • From zero to comfortable with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Version control, project setup, and debugging rituals
    • Studio-style feedback cycles with your instructor
    • Building a personal learning dashboard inside the academy

  2. Weeks 3–6 · Applied projects

    Portfolio builds

    Ship three real‑world, cross‑discipline projects

    • Responsive marketing site for a fictional product
    • Design system tokens & reusable UI library
    • Product analytics dashboard with real data
    • Weekly live critique and refactoring sessions

  3. Weeks 7–9 · Deep dives

    Specialization

    Pick a focus: engineering, product design, or growth

    • Engineering: APIs, auth, performance and deployment
    • Design: research, prototyping, and design reviews
    • Growth: funnels, lifecycle campaigns, and experimentation
    • 1:1 mentor office hours tailored to your path

  4. Weeks 10–12 · Launch

    Career support

    Demo day, career storytelling, and next steps

    • Polish your portfolio and GitHub for interviews
    • Asynchronous video feedback on your presentations
    • Mock interviews and salary negotiation workshops
    • Graduation, alumni access, and ongoing learning paths
