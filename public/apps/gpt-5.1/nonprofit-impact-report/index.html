Hope, age 11 – finally back at school

Rural Kenya · Education access · Scholarship + school meals

When drought forced Hope's family to relocate, school was the first thing she lost. Through your support, she received a scholarship, a backpack filled with supplies, and a hot meal every day. Her attendance is now at 98%, and she dreams of becoming a nurse in her community.

Her class saw a 42% increase in literacy in one year. 1 of 12,600 students supported this year

A clinic that arrived on four wheels

Northern India · Mobile health · Maternal & child care

Before the mobile clinic, the nearest doctor was a day's travel away. Now, trained nurses visit Kamala's village twice a month, providing checkups, vaccines, and prenatal screenings. In the last year alone, the mobile clinic delivered over 300 healthy babies in remote communities.

Infant mortality in her district dropped by 18%. 47,000+ clinic visits funded by donors
“

From one seedling to a thriving farm

Peru · Climate‑smart agriculture · Women-led cooperatives

Rosa joined a training group with just a small plot of land. With new irrigation techniques and drought-resistant crops, she transformed her harvest, doubled her income, and now mentors other women farmers. Her cooperative reinvests profits into community savings and school fees.

Average household income in her village grew by 27%. 1,350+ micro‑entrepreneurs launched this year
