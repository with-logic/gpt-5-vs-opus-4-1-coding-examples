Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a typical box? ‚ñº

Each box contains 8-10 varieties of seasonal produce, typically including a mix of vegetables, fruits, and herbs. Contents vary by season to provide the freshest possible produce.

Can I customize my box? ‚ñº

While we don't offer full customization, you can add optional items like eggs, flowers, and bread. We also accommodate common allergies with advance notice.

How does delivery work? ‚ñº

We offer both home delivery and pickup at several convenient locations. Delivery is available within 15 miles of the farm. You can pause or skip deliveries anytime.

Is everything organic? ‚ñº

We use organic farming practices and are certified organic. All produce is grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.
