Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in each box? ▼

Each box contains 8-12 varieties of fresh, seasonal produce. The exact contents vary week to week based on what's ready for harvest. You'll receive a mix of vegetables, fruits, and herbs, typically enough for a family of 2-4 for a week.

Can I customize my box? ▼

While we can't customize individual boxes, we offer a swap table at pickup locations where members can trade items. We also provide recipes and storage tips for unfamiliar produce to help you make the most of your share.

What if I'm going on vacation? ▼

You can pause your subscription with 48 hours notice, or donate your box to our community food bank. Many members also arrange for friends to pick up their share while they're away.

Are your farms organic? ▼

Yes! All our partner farms use organic growing practices. While not all are certified organic (due to cost), they follow strict guidelines for sustainable, chemical-free farming.
